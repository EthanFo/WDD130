This cycle consists of four stages: cue, craving, response, and reward. First, a cue triggers the habit. Next, a craving arises, motivating a specific response. This response provides a reward, satisfying the desire associated with the cue. Over time, this neurological feedback loop solidifies the habit. To break free, consider these strategies:

- Quit Cold Turkey: While challenging, abstaining entirely from the bad habit can prove your capability for change.

- Change Your Habit Loop: Overwrite the old habit with a new routine. Strengthen the new behavior to make it more resilient against relapses.
